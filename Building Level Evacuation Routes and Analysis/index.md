# 1. Downloading Building Footprints (Open Buildings V3)

The Open Buildings V3 dataset contains **1.8 billion building detections** across an inference area of **58 million km²**, covering:

- Africa  
- South Asia  
- South-East Asia  
- Latin America  
- The Caribbean  

The building footprint data is derived from the **Google Open Buildings dataset (v3)**,  
© Google, licensed under **CC BY 4.0**.
